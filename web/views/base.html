Photography

Our commitment to excellence is our number one asset. We are focused on giving you quality service at reasonable prices. We intend to give you the full digital copies of your photo sessions so that you can decide what to do with them. They are YOUR memories and you deserve to have them all.

I specialize in outdoor portrait, sports, birthday and wedding photography. While shooting portraits are great, I love to capture the moment of an event. Contact us for candid wedding and birthday packages.

We also have the capabilities to restore old photos. Restoration prices begin at $20.00 and increase with the severity of damage to the photo. Unfortunately, every photo cannot be restored.

Media

Our media focus is on creating slide shows that attempt to recreate the memories of special moments in your life. In the past we have created slide shows for graduations, weddings, and even funerals. (Slide show prices vary based on the number of photos and length.) We can also turn your old VHS tapes into DVDs or better yet, video files. This type of service is one that I do not take lightly. I know how precious your videos are and we handle them with the utmost care.

*We do not keep your files on our server. Once the files are converted and checked for accuracy they are removed. You are responsible for your DVD or Mpeg file.

Design

As a design team we will work tirelessly to develop a logo for you that is original and informational. Your company’s logo should be recognizable at a glance. At el Swann Design will strive to create a logo that makes your company stand out from the crowd. Design Pricing: Please remember that no project is the same. Every logo and client requires a different amount of work. We can’t give you an accurate quote until we know exactly what you need created. Prices start as low as $99.00 but increase with time and layouts.
